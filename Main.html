About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is a premier industry-led training and deployment institute. We focus exclusively on cultivating talent for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles within regulated, high-consequence environments.

We bridge the gap between theory and execution. By emphasizing the critical link between academic insight and real-world application, we ensure our practitioners are ready to tackle tomorrow's threats today.

Academic Excellence Meets Industry Reality

Partnered with leading UK universities, our curriculum translates cutting-edge academic research directly into actionable, real-world projects and deployments for our partners.

Our Service Pillars

Academy

Intensive training and bootcamps designed for both students and seasoned professionals. We build the foundational and advanced skills required to manage digital risk at scale.

Innovation & Incubation

Driving the future of security. We actively develop proprietary IP, formulate future-proof risk models, and establish governance frameworks for emerging technologies like AI.

Advisory Services

Expert consulting to help organizations navigate complex compliance landscapes. We guide the implementation of critical frameworks including NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2.

Who We Serve

Our community is built on excellence and resilience. We cater to students, early-career professionals, and established practitioners seeking rigorous upskilling in digital and cyber risk.

Our deployed talent primarily serves critical sectors—including Financial Services and National Critical Infrastructure—where failure is not an option and proactive risk management is a core business driver.
