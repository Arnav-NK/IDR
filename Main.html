01 About IDR

Where rigorous academic insight meets the demands of industry

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute purpose-built for digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments. We exist at the intersection of education and practice — equipping individuals with the frameworks, tools and real-world experience that today's organisations urgently need.

Through a formal partnership with leading UK universities, IDR draws on the latest academic research while grounding every programme in live industry projects. Graduates don't just learn theory — they emerge deployment-ready, having worked on genuine risk challenges alongside experienced practitioners.
